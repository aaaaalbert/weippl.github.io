<h3>Master's Thesis - Organizational Information</h3>

								<p>You will need to take some courses in IT security (at TU) and at least one 'praktikum' with me before you can do a Master's thesis with me.
								Your thesis needs to be written in English. <br>
								<b>Please look at <a href="http://owl.english.purdue.edu/owl/">Purdue's Online Writing Lab</a></b>. It is a great resource!'</p>


								<p>Please prepare an <b> abstract </b> for the thesis that contains following items
								<ul style="list-style-type:square;">
									<li> Problem statement and Goal of the work</li>
									<li> Expected outcome / results </li>
									<li> Research methodology </li>
									<li> Preliminary schedule and planned workpackages </li>
									<li> State-of-the art (including at least 4 references to relevant research) </li>
								</ul>
								</p>

								 <b>Minimum standards  </b> for a passing grade (in addition to standards set forth by the Dean):
								<ul style="list-style-type:square;">
									<li> Please use <a href="http://slimtimer.com/">http://slimtimer.com/</a> or an excel sheet to record your working hours. This will give you (and me) a better overview whether you work efficiently on your thesis...</li>
									<li> approx. 25000 words</li>
									<li> min. working hours 750 (30 ECTS)</li>
									<li> References. Please use ACM (<a href="http://www.acm.org/publications/latex_style/">http://www.acm.org/publications/latex_style/</a>),
									IEEE (<a href="http://www2.computer.org/portal/web/cscps/formatting">http://www2.computer.org/portal/web/cscps/formatting</a>) or
									APA style (<a href="http://owl.english.purdue.edu/owl/resource/560/05/">http://owl.english.purdue.edu/owl/resource/560/05/</a>). Personally I prefer ACM but all three are fine.</li>
									<li>Please look at Prof. Werthner's checklist (<a href="http://www.ec.tuwien.ac.at/ec/teaching/master_topics/guide">http://www.ec.tuwien.ac.at/ec/teaching/master_topics/guide</a>)</li>
